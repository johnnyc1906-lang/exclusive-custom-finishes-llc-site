What We Offer

Our Flooring Services

From installation to restoration, we offer a comprehensive range of flooring services to meet every need and style preference.

🌳
Hardwood Flooring

Installation, refinishing, and repair of solid and engineered hardwood floors. We match existing stains and finishes perfectly for seamless results.

◾
Luxury Vinyl Plank & Tile

Waterproof, durable LVP and LVT flooring that mimics the look of wood or stone. Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic areas.

⬜
Tile & Stone Installation

Expert installation of ceramic, porcelain, marble, and natural stone tile for floors, showers, and feature walls. Precision cutting and grouting included.

🌟
Floor Refinishing & Restoration

Bring worn, dull, or scratched floors back to life. Our refinishing process removes old finishes and applies a fresh, beautiful coat for a like-new appearance.

📱
Laminate Flooring

Cost-effective laminate solutions that deliver stunning aesthetics. We offer a wide range of styles, textures, and finishes to complement any décor.

🕵
Custom Staining & Finishing

Unique staining, bleaching, and custom color mixing to create one-of-a-kind flooring finishes that make your space truly exclusive and personal.
